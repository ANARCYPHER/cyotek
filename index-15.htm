About Us
We offer a fully customized, fully hosted, and liquidity ready cryptocurrency trading platform which is real world ready and tested since 2013. Our platform allows our partners to start a cryptocurrency exchange without having to invest in developing or operating the back-end software and infrastructure.

Our mission is to develop and maintain an industry-leading, fully customizable, white label cryptocurrency exchange platform with shared liquidity. The white label service will simultaneously provide our partners with a straightforward and secure exchange platform while ensuring end users a fast, smooth, and intuitive trading experience.

  • Fully Customized
    Our solution supports any language, currency, and it is tailored to fit each target market’s KYC/AML laws and regulations. Integration with payment service providers and third-party services.
  • Fully Hosted
    BTCTrader’s white label exchange platform is off­ered entirely as a service, meaning our partners will not have any technical and infrastructural responsibilities.
  • Liquidity Ready
    Our platform comes equipped with liquidity ‘in-hand’ on crypto-crypto pairs meaning that our partner’s exchanges have access to a dynamic order book from the moment their site goes live.
  • Secure & Scalable
    BTCTrader provides extensive front-end and back-end security measures. Conducting regular penetration tests and audits with the credible firms. The cloud hosting option provides high availability and smooth auto-scaling management for consistent user experience.
BTCTrader Network Our aim is to build the “BTCTrader Network” by sharing the robust infrastructure & liquidity to every market around the world, through local partnerships. With the proven back-end solution, our partners will not consume any added time, effort or budget and this will allow them to concentrate on growing their business in the target market.

Achieving the objective of hosting multiple secure, compliant, and liquid exchanges within, being a part of it would be beneficial for all parties by jointly empowering the network.
EASY MIGRATION FOR EXISTING EXCHANGES We offer the white label service also to the existing exchanges. By understanding the needs and requirements of our prospective partners, we provide them with a smooth transition process relying on our robust solution. Besides the infrastructural improvement, being a part of the BTCTrader Network will grant access to a liquid network.
